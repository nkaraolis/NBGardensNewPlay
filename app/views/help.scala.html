NG Gardens' Help & Support
Sign up for NB Gardens account

It's free to create an NB Gardens account.
Visit the NB Gardens registration page.
Follow the steps on the screen to complete your account setup.
After you've created your NB Gardens Account, you can use it to sign in to NB Gardens and make purchases.

Q: The username I want is taken

You won't be able to get an NB Gardens address if:
The exact username you requested is already being used.
The username was used by someone in the past, and they deleted their account.

Q: How do I edit my account details?

Visit your account page.
Click the edit account details link.
Follow the steps on the screen to complete the changes to your account.
After saving these changes you are now able to resume making your purchases with ease.

Q: How do I search and browse for items?

If you're looking for a specific item, enter a few words into the search box on the NB Gardens home page and we'll show you all the matching results.

Note: The only information we have about a particular item is what we display on the product detail page for that item. Details usually include customer reviews, which can sometimes provide helpful information about content and product compatibility.
